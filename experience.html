I started working at Trio by Jerasia located at SACC Mall when I was 18 years old which is January 2019. I worked there for almost a year before I decided to pursue my study at UiTM. And then, I started to work there again temporarily during my semester break.

Trio by Jerasia is a retail store where customers can find casual clothing with very affordable price. My salary there started from RM 1,200 with OT bonus and commission. My position there are as a cashier and also promoter.

All the staffs there are very helpful and nice. While working there, I get to learn time management, improve my social skills, responsibility and trust. I also got to experience the difficulty of being a retail worker where I need to face many types of customers.

During my third semester break, I decided to work at hijab booth located at SACC Mall also. Working there is such a fun experience for me as the work is pretty easy and stress-free. My salary there also start at RM 1,200.
